What is a Coupa delegate?

A delegate is a user who is authorized to act in place of another Coupa user, typically for approvals, purchase requests, or task completion during absences.

In Coupa, delegation makes sure work continues when someone is out of office. A delegate can approve requisitions, submit requests, or take actions on behalf of the original user, depending on the permissions assigned by the primary user or administrator.

  • Delegation is often used for approvers who will be unavailable for a period of time.
  • Delegates should be trusted users with similar approval authority.
  • The original user can add or remove delegates from their profile settings.

How to assign a delegate

Use the user delegation settings in Coupa to give a colleague the ability to approve or submit transactions on your behalf.

The process of assigning a delegate in Coupa involves a few simple steps:

  • Open your Coupa profile and go to the Delegates section.
  • Select 'Create'.
  • Fill in all required information.
  • Select 'Save'.
