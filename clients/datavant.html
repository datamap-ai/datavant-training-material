👤 User Space

All things profile!

Navigate & configure your profile settings to manage your Coupa experience.

Accessing the profile options is straightforward. Follow these steps to learn how navigate and configure your profile:

  • Select your initials in the top right window frame.
  • Select the secondary "Settings" link option.
  • That's it! You should now see your profile settings page.

The Profile settings within Coupa houses a variety of functional tabs! Lets take a brief look at what each entails.

  • General - User forms, Profile Photo, and Password resetting.
  • Address Book - Set your Address.
  • Content - View Tagged Object & manage your Product Reviews.
  • Delegates - Assign & manage your Delegates.
  • Billing Accounts - Configure & view your accounts.
  • Reports - Manage your Scheduled Reports, and view your report log.
  • Notifications - Configure your Email, System/Mobile Notifications.
  • Multi Factor Authentication: - Setup and manage your MFA tied to the account.

🤝 Team Synergy

Creating delegates in Coupa

Understand how to create a delegate and the requirements to do so safely.

Delegates allow trusted colleagues to act on your behalf when you are unavailable. This ensures approvals and purchasing actions continue without interruption.

  • Delegates should have the right level of authority for the tasks they will perform.
  • Type settings determine the scope of actions a delegate can perform.
  • Delegation stays in place until the specified end date or until the primary user removes it.

📊 Activity

Lifecycle tracking and management

Monitor and manage your procurement lifecycle with comprehensive visibility.

Coupa's Activity dashboard empowers users with comprehensive operational visibility, making document management clean and accessible:

  • Central Hub Management: View, review, and organize all your active or historical requisitions, invoices, purchase orders, and more in one unified layout.
  • Real-Time Status Indicators: Instantly verify exactly where a document sits in the processing lifecycle (e.g., Draft, Pending Approval, or Fully Approved).
  • Bottleneck Visibility: Pinpoint precisely which management personnel currently holds an approval request to ensure timely follow-ups.

Navigate guided buying in Coupa

Learn how guided buying helps users in a variety of automated workflow submissions.

Guided buying in Coupa gives users a curated experience. See below for more information on Datavant's system routes:

  • Request an NDA (For supplier evaluation processes) & Datavant Contract Templates.
  • Initiate New Sourcing Requests and vendor evaluations.
  • Instantiate Contract Requests (NDA / MSA / SOW / BAA / DPA / Amendments).
  • Submit structural Purchase Requisitions for management validation.
  • Submit valid PO Change Requests for existing contracts.
